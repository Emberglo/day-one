About Ryan

Ryan was born in San Diego, California and has lived in San Juan, Puerto Rico and Chicago, Illinois. He relocated to Boise 10 years ago to attend Boise State University for Wildlife Biology. Through some odd chance, he ended up managing retail stores and hating it. He started looking for a new career path. Ryan's father has been a programer and computer system engineer for his entire career and so, Ryan had a natural interest in technology.

Outside of work, Ryan is a semi-professional wildlife photographer, fly-fisherman, master falconer, and avid explorer. Ryan travels as often as possible and has visited 5 of the 7 continents and hopes to complete the set.
